\f0\fs24 \cf0 # Beaver Simulation\
\
## Concept\
A minimal 2D simulation game where a player-controlled beaver gathers wood from trees and builds a dam to raise the water level around its lodge, protecting itself from predators.\
\
## Core Loop\
Move \uc0\u8594  gather wood \u8594  build dam \u8594  raise water level \u8594  block or slow predators \u8594  survive\
\
## Goal\
Build a small but playable prototype over a weekend that tests my ability to develop a game quickly and could serve as a foundation for a larger future game.\
\
## In Scope\
- 1 player-controlled beaver\
- 1 map\
- 1 resource: wood\
- Trees as resource nodes\
- Dam building\
- Water level increases as the dam grows\
- 1 predator that tries to reach the beaver\
- Water slows the predator first, and very high water blocks it\
- Lose condition when predator reaches beaver\
\
## Out of Scope\
- AI beavers\
- Multiple maps\
- Complex crafting\
- Advanced water physics\
- Polished graphics\
- Menus/settings}
